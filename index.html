Austin Haedicke, MA, LAPC

I am a Licensed Associate Professional Counselor providing play and individual therapy services to children and adolescents (ages 4 - 18 years old) under direction and supervision at Out-of-the Box Solutions, Inc.

I work primarily from a person / child centered perspective and employ play and narrative therapy techniques, however, I am adamant about adapting to the needs of my clients and utilize a variety of other practices when the need is necessitated.

Counseling and Psychotherapy

Psychotherapy is an inherently interpersonal and relational process that often involves deep exploration, acceptance of, and regulation of the vast dynamics of the human psyche.

I focus on using play therapy, narrative, psychodynamic, and other expressive techniques. However, I also punctuate treatment with an emphasis on meeting the client's present need which often requires a wide arrary of technical approches to work with distressing issues such as:

  • + Trauma, Sexual Abuse, Physical Abuse, Neglect
  • + Anger, Anxiety, Depression, Fear, Emotional Instability,
  • + Attachment and Separation, Divorce, Death, and Grief

About Me

I have worked with children and adolescents in a variety of settings including inpatient and partial hospitalization as well as outpatient environments. I believe that play is the language of children and so it is the primary medium for assisting them in working through their difficulties.

Since 2014 I have been an active member in the American Academy of Psychotherapists, and in 2015 received my Master of Arts degree in Psychology from the University of West Georgia. Currently I am pursuing licensure as a professional counselor in the State of Georiga, as well as certification as a play therapist through The Association for Play Therapy.
